About Us

The Burbank High School Machine Learning Club is new beginner-friendly club. We welcome anyone who is curious about AI and data science. We meet every other week during lunch for hands-on activities, mini hackathons, workshops, and fun projects. No experience needed, just bring your curiosity and enthusiasm to learn and create together!
