About Us

Welcome to the BHS Machine Learning Club! We are a new beginner-friendly club for anyone who is curious about AI and data science. We meet every other week during lunch for hands-on activities, mini hackathons, workshops, and fun projects. No experience needed, just bring your curiosity and enthusiasm to learn and create together!
